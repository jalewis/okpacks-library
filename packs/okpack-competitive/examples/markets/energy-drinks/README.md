# Sample market: energy drinks — the new-entrant kit

Framed for someone **starting an energy-drink brand**: the watchlist splits the market into the
shelf you're fighting for (Red Bull / Monster / Rockstar), your direct challenger cohort
(Celsius, Alani Nu, Ghost, C4 — the better-for-you wave that proved entry is possible), and
adjacent brand-leverage entrants (Prime, ZOA). `home: my-energy-brand` is the anchor — rename it
to your brand and `discover-competitors` builds co-occurrence around YOUR position.

What the vault answers as it compounds: who owns which segment and channel, what distribution
deals are being signed (the #1 entry barrier), which functional claims are trending, where the
whitespace is (flavor/function/channel combos nobody holds), and — via predictions — dated,
falsifiable calls on category moves.

Setup: copy `feeds.opml` → `feeds/feeds.opml`, `watchlist.yaml` → `config/competitive-watchlist.yaml`, deploy.
