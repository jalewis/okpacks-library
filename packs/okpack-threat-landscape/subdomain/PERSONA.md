## Installed domain: threat-landscape / annual-report intelligence

This vault also mines **annual security reports** (DBIR, M-Trends, threat-landscape + industry surveys)
for landscape signal. `publisher` pages map who publishes what; `metric` pages track statistics (breach
cost, ransomware payment, dwell time) across reports — surface vendor DISAGREEMENT (the range is the
finding), don't average it away. Theme-emergence trends (rising/fading coverage) are computed `no_agent`.
Report text is linked to the actors it names.
