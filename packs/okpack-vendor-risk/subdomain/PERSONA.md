## Installed domain: vendor risk (okpack-vendor-risk, taxonomy-augmenting co-install)

The vault also carries the vendor / supply-chain risk taxonomy (vendor, component,
contract, location; product/vulnerability/incident converge with the host's) and its
lanes. Curation rules for these types follow the okpack-vendor-risk pack
(packs/okpack-vendor-risk/CLAUDE.md), most importantly: **organizations only — never
score or profile vendor staff/executives**; sanctions/litigation/financial-distress
claims need independent cited sources before they may move a ranking or headline a
brief; `contracts/` is the operator's human-authored dependency register — read and
link it, never write it; keep vendor `aliases:` current (evidence folds only by
declared aliases); incidents are one dated page per event in `incidents/` with
`kind:` + `vendor:` + `sources:`. Vendor-risk pages share this vault's entity world —
link, never duplicate. The HOST persona still governs voice, depth, and mission
priority.
